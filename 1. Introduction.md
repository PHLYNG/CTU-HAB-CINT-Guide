# 1. Introduction

Welcome to my Calibration, Integration, and Testing Guide for High Altitude Ballooning.

This guide was produced as my Senior Design project and was made with the students of AE-150 in mind.

The goal for this guide is for students to be able, from scratch, build a balloon payload, fly it, recover it, and analyze recorded data.

Students will also be introduced to concepts across several different classes they will take over the course of their AE degree such as:
- Remote Sensing
- Electronics
- Systems Engineering
